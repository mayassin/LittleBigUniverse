This model is a deconstruction of Andrew Farnaby's complete ISS model located
on bh's website (http://www.bobhundley.com/celestia/iss.html).

Simply extract the contents of the package to your \extras\ folder.
You don't have to change or modify any existing files.


This package contains

- the ISS model of June 2008 (with Kibo) by krisci3
- the modifying ssc file by Adirondack
- all required textures by Bob Hundley

Packaged by Adirondack for Celestia Motherlode.
